1. Purpose: The credit policy ensures that all lending activities comply with regulatory standards and minimize financial risk. 
2. Scope: This policy applies to all personal loans, business loans, and credit card offerings.
3. Approval: All credit applications must undergo risk assessment and approval by the credit committee.
4. Documentation: Required documents include identity proof, income proof, and collateral details if applicable.
5. Review: Credit policies are reviewed annually to align with changes in market conditions and regulatory requirements.
